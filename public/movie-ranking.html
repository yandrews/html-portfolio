The Best Manga According to Yusef

My top 3 Manga of all-time

Slam Dunk

Every now and then I come across a manga that becomes my new favorite #2. The reason why #1 will never be replaced is because Slam Dunk wraps three of my favorite interest in one, basketball, manga and comedy.

D-Frag

The characters in this comedy are full of flavor and I can't get enough of the goofy character and straight man combo.

Dorohedoro

Love the characters and art style. It takes a comedic slice of life take on action, gothic, gory action.
